Welcome to the least-cool dot-cool on the Internet

2017/10/23

I registered the seth.cool domain a couple of years ago with no project in mind – only the misguided assumptions that (1) someone else would buy it, and (2) that I am cool. I’ve decided to finally use my first-mover’s advantage to share data-related projects as I work on them.

A brief background on that: As my job has evolved to include more and more data analysis, I’ve found myself spending a lot of time waiting for Excel to unfreeze respond. A longtime friend of mine suggested that I learn R, and shared two textbooks (R Cookbook and R Graphics Cookbook). These, Hadley Wickham and Garret Grolemund’s R for Data Science, along with several DataCamp, Coursera, and edX courses have me well on my way.

I plan to improve upon the aesthetics of this site in the future, which is built by Yihui Xie’s new blogdown package. In the meantime, I’ll be honing my skills on the most interesting (to me) datasets I can find, and sharing my practice here.

I can be contacted via email. Suggestions, corrections, and feedback are appreciated.
